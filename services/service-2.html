Fisgard Capital
Corporation

Since 1994, Fisgard Capital Corporation (FCC) has enabled investors to earn income from a diversified, stable and growing portfolio of mortgages, primarily first mortgages, secured by valuable Canadian real estate property.

To preserve capital and provide consistent, reliable returns to our investors, FCC focuses on the underserved market for borrowers, offering timely service, flexible financing terms, and competitive products.
