Who We Are

Fisgard Asset Management Corporation has been locally owned and operated in Victoria, BC, since 1994. Our name pays tribute to the historic Fisgard Lighthouse, which symbolizes security, stability and vision—values we share with our clients and that guide every aspect of our operations.

2600+ Investors

A client-focused, independent Mortgage Investment Manager as well as an Exempt Market Dealer, Fisgard offers investment products to thousands of investors from British Columbia to Ontario.

Flexible Solutions

Fisgard provides borrowers with residential and commercial private mortgage solutions, from residential first and second mortgages to multi-million dollar commercial, construction and mezzanine loans.

Respected Specialists

Fisgard has grown to become one of Canada’s most respected mortgage investment managers specifically focused on Mortgage Investment Corporations (MICs).

Our Vision

From the start, Fisgard has focused on making investing more accessible for people of all ages and income levels. Investors can start small, investing just $1000, allowing students and young people to begin building bright financial futures. We are equally focused on helping mortgage professionals find the right solution for borrowers, making it possible for Canadians to purchase their dream home, make necessary renovations and build wealth.
